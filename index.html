How to Do a Yes or No Tarot Reading

1. Ask a Clear Question

Frame your question so it can be answered with yes or no. Be specific. Instead of "Will I be happy?" ask "Will accepting this job bring fulfillment?"

2. Focus Your Intention

Take a breath and concentrate on your question. The clearer your focus, the more meaningful the card's message will be.

3. Draw Your Card

Click to reveal your card. Each of the 78 tarot cards carries a yes, no, or maybe energy based on centuries of tarot tradition.

4. Read the Answer

Your card will show a clear yes, no, or maybe answer plus deeper guidance about your situation including love and career insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this yes or no tarot reading accurate?

Online tarot tools provide guidance based on traditional card meanings assigned over centuries. For the most personal readings, we recommend using a physical tarot deck. Browse our tarot deck collection or try a dedicated yes or no oracle deck.

How many cards are in a tarot deck?

A standard tarot deck contains 78 cards: 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana. This tool includes all 78 with complete meanings. Learn more in our complete tarot card meanings guide.

What is the difference between oracle cards and tarot?

Tarot follows a fixed 78-card structure. Oracle cards are more flexible with no set rules. Both are powerful divination tools. Read our what are oracle cards guide for a complete comparison.

Can I do a yes or no reading with oracle cards?

Yes! Dedicated yes or no oracle decks make it even simpler because each card is explicitly labeled yes, no, or maybe. No interpretation needed.
